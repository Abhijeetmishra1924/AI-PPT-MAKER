# AI-PPT-MAKER
AI PPT Maker – Deep Corporate Presentation Generator
A fast, AI-powered Google Colab tool that instantly creates professional, research-depth PowerPoint decks (10–12 slides) on any topic.
Powered by Groq's ultra-fast Llama-3.3-70B model and python-pptx, this tool generates rich, corporate-style presentations with mixed-length content — combining concise paragraphs, detailed explanations, and bullet points for a natural and engaging flow.
Ideal for consultants, executives, researchers, analysts, or anyone who needs a high-quality deck in minutes.
Key Features

Deep, structured content: Automatically generates 10–12 slides covering:
Title Slide
Executive Summary
Background & Context
Key Applications
Benefits & Impact
Market Landscape
Technical Approach
Real-world Case Study
Challenges & Ethics
Future Outlook
Conclusion & Recommendations
